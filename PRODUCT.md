brand

## Users
Students participating in the DS2 table football tournament. They are accessing the site to quickly find event details and register their teams. They need a fast, zero-friction experience.

## Product Purpose
A highly punchy, ultra-minimal registration page and a simple admin panel to manage signups for a table football tournament. It functions as an interactive event poster.

## Brand Personality
Event poster, immediate, punchy, zero fluff, athletic, confident, effortless to scan. 

## Anti-references
- Generic SaaS aesthetics
- Cluttered forms with excessive helper text
- The rounded 2023 AI look (glassmorphism, overly rounded corners)
- Anything with unnecessary words or paragraphs

## Design Principles
- Whitespace is the primary layout tool. Sections breathe. Nothing feels cramped.
- Let icons, numbers, and visual hierarchy do the work that words would otherwise do.
- No tooltips, no helper text, no descriptions under headings.
- Rules, info, and form fields use icons as anchors — the label IS the content.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- High contrast for readability against the deep green background.
- Clean, readable typography (Bebas Neue for headings, Inter for body).
